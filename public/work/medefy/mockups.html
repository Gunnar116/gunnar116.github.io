Care guide chat workflow
Members reach a real person, with AI doing the warm-up.
Texoma connects members with their care guides through chat — AI handles the first moments of a conversation, then hands off cleanly to a human when judgment is needed.
1
Member opens a chat.
AI greets, listens, and frames the question.
2
AI hands off to a human care guide.
Context, signals, and history travel with the conversation.
3
Care guide resolves and follows up.
Status is visible to the member at every step.
